FORMAT OF EACH FILE:
[There could be exceptions, but i tried to capture the general trend through each files]
(TV) -> stands for any: TV, V, VG, etc.
* repeated 0, 1, or more times
~ optional attribute
a space can also be a tab...

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CERTIFICATES:

<Title (Year) (TV)* Country:Rating (Detail)*>


example:
Haute tension (2003) South Korea:18 (re-rating) (cut) (DVD rating) (uncut)


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GENRES:

<Title (Year) (TV)* Genre>

example:
Akujo no kamen (1980) (TV) Crime


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEYWORDS (part 1):

<Keyword (Quantity)>

example:
cyberculture (8)


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEYWORDS (part 2):

<Title (Year) (TV)* Keyword>

example:
Deep Impact (1998) yacht


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MOVIES:

<Title (Year) (TV)* Year2>

note: 
Year and Year2 usually identical but not always.

example:
Jane (1982) 1982


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RATINGS:

<Distribution Votes Rank Title (Year) (TV)*>

note:
I find the voting system difficult to understand apart that Rank goes from 1 (bad) to 10 (good).

example:
1...002112   28    7.4   Goodnight Moon (2001)


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RELEASE DATES:

<Title (Year) (TV)* Country:Date (Detail)*>

note:
most movies are released same year as produced. But sometimes they are released later (ex: in a different country - see below)

example:
Enemy (2013) Hungary:10 October 2015 (TV premiere)


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RUNNING TIMES:

<Title (Year) (TV)* ~Country:~Minutes (Detail)*>

examples:
Warlords (2007) (TV) Netherlands:280 (4 parts) (DVD)
Yukiko (2011) 15
